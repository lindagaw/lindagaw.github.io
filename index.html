Bio:

My name is Ashley Gao, a 4th year phd student at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia. My advisor is professor John Stankovic.

Before joining professor Stankovic's group, I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science and B.A. in Literatures of the World from the University of California, San Diego in 2017. I graduated with department honors of high distinction on my B.A., advised by professor Page duBois.

My research interests are affective computing and transfer learning.

Research Overview:

My research direction lies in the field of transfer learning and domain adaptation, as well as the intersection of deep learning and smart health.
