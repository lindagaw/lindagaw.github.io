My name is Ashley Gao, and I am an Assistant Professor at the College of William & Mary. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Virginia (UVa), advised by professor John Stankovic . My research interest lie in the intersection of domain generalization, transfer learning, and how to use such techniques in applications in the cyber physical systems, especially in the settings of smart healthcare. Before joining Professor Stankovic's group, I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science at the University of Virginia, my B.S. in Computer Science and B.A. in Literatures of the World at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD).

Research Assistant Recruitment

I am actively looking for graduate and undergraduate students from the following disciplinary backgrounds: computer science, data science, artificial intelligence, information science, health sciences.

If you are interested in working with me, please feel free to email me following the instructions below. In your email, please include:

  • Your resume/CV.
  • Your current degree program, and a brief overview of any relevant coursework or research experience you have.
  • A concise and pithy summarization on why you are interested in working with me; please refer to my publications for the works that I have been doing. Note that if you are a Master's or undergraduate student, I may direct you to partake in an independent study project before considering providing you a funded position.
