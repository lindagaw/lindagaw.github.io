Bio:

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Virginia working with Professor John Stankovic. My research interests are in the areas of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS), including the Internet-of-Things (IoT), wearables and ubiquitous computing, wireless sensing, human-computer interaction, and machine perception. In particular, my research proposes Vibration Intelligence (VibInt) in CPS, which is applied in a wide variety of areas, such as wearables interfaces, robotics, smart health, smart home, privacy and security. I am also a co-founder of VibInt AI, a startup working on vibration intelligence on wearable devices.
